Blissey

Blissey is a Normal-type Pokémon known for its exceptional healing abilities and nurturing nature. It evolves from Chansey when leveled up with high friendship. Blissey is often found in hospitals and care centers, where it uses its soft, fluffy body to comfort and heal injured Pokémon and humans alike. Its signature move, Heal Bell, allows it to cure status conditions of its allies, making it a valuable support Pokémon in battles.

  • Altura: 1,5m
  • Peso: 46,8kg
  • Categoría: Felicidad
  • Habilidad: Cura Natural
